    About Us

    Delivering High-Performance Textiles for a Everyone.

    Welcome to SRO Techtax, a leader in the innovative world of technical textiles. With a passion for excellence and a commitment to quality, we have been at the forefront of the textile industry, providing cutting-edge solutions to a diverse range of sectors.

    OUR STORY

    • Founded in 2019, SRO Techtax started with a vision to revolutionize the textile industry through technological advancements and sustainable practices. Over the years, we have grown from a small enterprise into a globally recognized brand, thanks to our dedication to research, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

    What We Do

    • At SRO Techtax, we specialize in the design, development, and manufacture of high-performance textiles for various applications. One of our standout innovations is Build Tech, which includes advanced textile solutions for architectural applications such as rooftops, facades, and interior designs. Our Build Tech products are engineered to provide exceptional durability, weather resistance, and aesthetic appeal, making them ideal for modern architectural projects.

    Why Choose Technical Textile

    Innovative and Functional: The Edge of Technical Textiles

    Choose technical textiles for their advanced functionality and innovative applications across diverse industries such as medical, automotive, and construction.

    Fire-safe

    • Almost all structural fabrics are flame retardant. Worldwide, the fire coding system checks on flammability, flame propagation or smoke.

    Low maintenance

    • Ideally technical textile materials & byproducts need to be annually maintained for its usage over an extended period of time. They require minimal maintenance as against an erected building or structure.

    Cost benefits

    • Technical textile structures are arguably low cost as against any conventional structure. It’s also reusable in certain cases and can be used to create multiple elements.

    Durability

    • If maintained properly with annual maintenance, technical textile architecture can be played out over a longer period.

    Flexible design aesthetics

    • Any structure can be custom-built as per specifications and provide the richness and aura as per requirement. Most materials are translucent to light that in turn, deliver an excellent finish.

    Acoustic

    • In recent years, technical fabrics have been created that help in reducing mid to high-level frequency.

    Lightweight

    • Due to its light weight, it requires minimal structural steel, thereby reducing overall costs.

    Translucent

    • Due to this property, structures in roofs invite natural light during day time, while at nights can light up with infused artificial lights.

    Sample materials for technical textile

    • PVC/ PVDF Coated Polyester Fabrics, PTFE coated fiberglass fabrics, ETFE film, Silicone coated Glass fabrics, to name just a few.

    Indoors

    • Although technical textile membrane architecture is predominantly used for the outdoors, it can even be used for sunshades, screens and ceilings.
