Protection of slopes by growing locally grown vegetation, in the form of turfing, is the best method to protect the slopes against the erosion because once the roots of this vegetation penetrate into the slope by 75-100 mm, they provide root reinforcement against the slope erosion
    • In such cases, the three-dimensional erosion control mats, called as Geomats, are useful as they increase the soilâ€™s resistance to erosion and some fertile soil layer can also be placed in these mats to promote growth of vegetation.
